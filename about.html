ABOUT US

Head Space and Sanity Kenya(HSSK) is raising awareness on the relevance of mental wellness especially among the youth in Kenya. Our youth face many challenges ranging from unemployment to pressure from both family and friends. We are working towards acquainting the youth on how to go about depression, putting on mind that it is normal to have both highs and lows and opening up to someone is the way to go.

VISION & MISSION

Our Vision

Head Space & Sanity Kenya (HSSK) believes that success in various aspects of life begins with a healthy mind. It views good mental health as a requisite for productivity and societal development. Our company visualizes a generation that is at liberty to express itself and reach out if need be for a better future.

Our Mission

Our mission as an organization is to create a platform for the youth to freely express themselves without experiencing any judgement, and to give them a chance to interact with their peers without feeling left out. For the youth, HSSK is like family.

Our Goals

  • To provide a platform for the youth to espress themselves without judgement.
  • To enable the youth to easily access proffesional counsellng services.
  • To create mental health awareness among the youth.
  • To improve youth's mental wellness and boost self-esteem.
